VISION

To develop a low cost use motion capture system that tracks motion of infrared markers through multiple video feeds.

Results

The Cheap Motion Capture System acheived most of the initially intended requirements. Even though the team encountered some technical obstacles, the main functionalities such as capturing video feed, filtering and extracting point data, processing the data in real time and then recreating the markers in virtual object.

Future

  • More than 4 Camera Support
  • Interactive GUI
  • 3D modeling software
